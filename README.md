# FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard – Excel & Power Query Project

This project focuses on analyzing the sales data of Ferns and Petals (FNP), a gifting platform that delivers products for various occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Birthdays, Anniversaries, and Valentine’s Day.
Using Excel and Power Query Editor, the dataset was cleaned, transformed, and modeled to build an interactive and insightful sales analysis dashboard.

## Project Overview

The goal of this project is to uncover meaningful insights around sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency using the FNP dataset.
The final dashboard helps stakeholders understand key patterns and make better business decisions.

## Key Objectives & Insights
1. Total Revenue
   
Calculated the overall revenue generated throughout 2023.

2. Average Order & Delivery Time

Analyzed how long orders take from placement to delivery, helping assess operational efficiency.

3. Monthly Sales Performance

Explored how revenue fluctuates month-wise, highlighting peak and low-sales months.

4. Top Products by Revenue

Identified highest revenue-generating products to prioritize stock and marketing.

5. Customer Spending Analysis

Evaluated the average spending per customer.

6. Sales Performance of Top 5 Products

Compared sales trends of the most profitable products.

7. Top 10 Cities by Orders

Determined cities placing the highest order volumes for targeted outreach.

8. Order Quantity vs. Delivery Time

Checked whether bulk orders impact delivery duration.

9. Revenue by Occasion

Compared revenue contributions from various occasions (Diwali, Anniversary, Birthday, Holi, etc.).

10. Product Popularity by Occasion

Found which products sell the most during specific festivals and events.
